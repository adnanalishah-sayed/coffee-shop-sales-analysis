# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Excel Project)

## Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop retail sales data using Microsoft Excel to generate actionable insights that support business decision-making and performance improvement.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze sales trends by day and hour
* Identify peak sales periods
* Evaluate monthly revenue performance
* Compare sales across store locations
* Calculate average order value per customer
* Identify best-selling products by quantity and revenue
* Analyze sales by product category and type

---

## Key Insights

* Sales are concentrated during specific peak hours
* Monthly revenue shows noticeable variation
* Store locations perform differently based on demand patterns
* Some products drive higher sales volume, while others generate higher revenue
* Certain product categories underperform and require attention

---

## Business Impact

* Improve staffing efficiency based on peak hours
* Increase revenue through upselling and bundling strategies
* Apply targeted campaigns during low-performing periods
* Focus on high-performing products and locations
* Enable data-driven decision-making

---

## Tools and Skills Used

* Microsoft Excel

  * Pivot Tables
  * Data Cleaning
  * Charts and Dashboards
  * Data Analysis

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used to transform raw data into meaningful insights, helping businesses improve efficiency, optimize sales strategies, and make informed decisions.
